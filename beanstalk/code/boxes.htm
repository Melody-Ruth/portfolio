Jack and the Beanstalk

Started: 2020

Last Updated: 2020

    

Instructions:

     Up arrow key to move forward. Down arrow key to move back. Left and right arrow keys to turn. Space to jump. Use the mouse to look around. Get coins to increase your score. If you fall too far you lose. When the sun sets the game is over.
